Creative Storytelling — Lipstick Brand (3 Formats)
Dialogue Script  ·  3 Reel Concepts  ·  5 Emotional Micro-Copies
“She cried. She healed. She reapplied. In that exact order.”
Part A — Three Shades at a Party (~35 Seconds)
Characters: Ruby Red (bold, dramatic, always late)  ·  Nude Pink (elegant, quietly observant)  ·  Midnight Berry (deep, emotional, unexpected heart) [Scene: Party in full swing. Nude Pink is already there, drink in hand.]
Ruby Red — enters fashionably late
“Okay — the party can officially start. I'm here.”
Nude Pink — completely unbothered
“You were 25 minutes late, Ruby.”
Ruby Red
“I don't arrive on time, darling. I arrive at the right moment.”
Midnight Berry — quietly by the window
“Some of us don't need an entrance. We're just… always here — for the people who matter.”
Ruby Red — clearly moved, clears throat
“I am NOT crying at a party —”
Midnight Berry
“You are absolutely crying.”
Ruby Red
“It's smudge-proof. I'm. Fine.”
[Brand End Card] “Three shades. Three personalities. One truth — your lipstick says everything before you do.” [Brand Name] — Wear Your Story.
Part B — 3 Scroll-Stopping Reel Concepts
Reel 01
“The Interview She Didn't Know She Had”
Hook: “She walked in wearing nude. Left the room wearing power.”
A woman applies nude lipstick in a washroom mirror before what looks like a high-stakes interview. Cut inside the boardroom — she's calm, articulate, commanding. Twist: the reverse angle reveals she wasn't being interviewed. She was the one hiring. Text overlay: “The shade doesn't change the room. It changes how you walk into it.” CTA: “Find your power shade. [Brand Name] — Link in bio.”
Reel 02
“Shade Match, But Make It a Mood Report”
Hook: “We don't match shades to skin tones. We match them to moods.”
Fast-cut montage — same woman, five situations (morning coffee, boardroom, post-breakup, girls' night, quiet Sunday). Each shot = different shade. Screen labels are mood tags, not colour names: • “Currently thriving and refusing to explain” — Deep Berry • “Not to be questioned before 10 AM” — Classic Red • “She said yes to herself” — Nude Zero voiceover. Just music, fast cuts, mood labels. CTA: “What's your mood today? Shop the collection.”
Reel 03
“The Lipstick Text Thread”
Hook: “When your lipstick has more to say than your ex.”
The entire reel is a fake phone message thread styled like WhatsApp. But the contacts are lipstick shade names: Ruby Red: “Don't text him back.” Nude Pink: “Hear him out, maybe?” Midnight Berry: “We. Are. Going. Out. Tonight. Final answer.” Her: “Okay I'm wearing all three. Bye.” 100% scroll-stopper. Instantly shareable. Zero production cost. CTA: “Some decisions are better made with your lipstick. [Brand Name] — Shop now.”
